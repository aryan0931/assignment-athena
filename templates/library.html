Roman Numerals

Origin: Ancient Rome

Base: 10 (Decimal) with sub-base 5

Uses letters (I, V, X, L, C, D, M) combined with additive and subtractive principles.

Mayan Numerals

Origin: Maya Civilization (Mesoamerica)

Base: 20 (Vigesimal)

A positional system using dots (1), bars (5), and a shell symbol for zero. Written vertically.

Babylonian Numerals

Origin: Ancient Mesopotamia

Base: 60 (Sexagesimal)

The oldest known positional system. Uses two symbols: a vertical wedge (1) and a corner wedge (10).

Chinese Numerals

Origin: Ancient China

Base: 10 (Decimal)

Uses characters for digits 0-9 and multipliers (10, 100, 1000, etc.). Highly regular structure.
